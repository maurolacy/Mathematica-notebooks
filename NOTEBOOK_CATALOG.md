# Notebook Catalog

Alphabetical listing of Mathematica notebooks with brief descriptions.

## A

- **`21-digit_Prime.nb`** - Finding the first 21-digit prime in the digits of e. Uses PrimePi and RiemannR function for estimation.

- **`a^b+b^a=i^2.nb`** - Perfect square test implementation using Jacobi symbols and modular arithmetic. Pre-computed tables for efficient square detection. *(Not original work - references StackExchange)*.

- **`ab+bc+ca==d2==e3.nb`** - Finding integer solutions where ab + bc + ca = d² = e³ using FindInstance.

- **`Archimedes.nb`** - Implementation of Archimedes' geometric approach to approximating π using polygons.

- **`AreaofEllipse.nb`** - Area of an ellipse calculations.

## B

- **`Borwein_integral.nb`** - Exploration of the curious Borwein integral formulas involving infinite products of sinc functions.

## C

- **`CircleCircleIntersection.nb`** - Algorithm for finding intersection points between two circles.

- **`Coronavirus.nb`** - Early (and surprisingly accurate) crude estimation of the number of deaths due to Covid-19 in Spain.

## E

- **`eq_Integers.nb`** - Solving Diophantine-style integer equations with constraints. Uses Solve and FindInstance.

- **`EulerSumofPowersConjecture.nb`** - Finding counterexamples to Euler's sum of powers conjecture (a⁴ + b⁴ + c⁴ = d⁴). Uses FindInstance to find integer solutions.

## F

- **`Fibonacci.nb`** - Fibonacci sequence calculations.

- **`fourier-series.nb`** - Fourier series analysis with coefficient extraction and visualization.

- **`FunctionalEquations.nb`** - Solving functional equations, including the Quora problem: "If f(x) + 2f(1/x) + 3f(x/(x-1)) == x, what is f(x)?"

## H

- **`HarmonicOscillator.nb`** - Harmonic oscillator physics calculations.

- **`HypersphereVolume.nb`** - Evolution of the volume formula for n-dimensional hyperspheres.

## I

- **`IntegralofDifference.nb`** - Integral of the difference of two functions.

- **`interferencePatterns.nb`** - Physics visualization of interference patterns using Fourier analysis.

## M

- **`MagicSquare.nb`** - Magic square generation and analysis.

- **`Mordell.nb`** - Computation and analysis of Mordell's integral.

## N

- **`nearestPointToPolygon.nb`** - Geofence algorithm for finding the nearest point to a polygon.

## O

- **`onesDigit.nb`** - Computing the ones digit of (√2 + √3)²⁰¹⁴ with high precision (1100 digits).

## P

- **`Palindromic.nb`** - Counting palindromic numbers less than 10ⁿ. Includes formulas for even/odd digit cases.

- **`PeriodDigits.nb`** - Analysis of the number of decimal digits in the period of 1/n.

- **`piApproximations.nb`** - Various methods for approximating π, including "Pi instant" calculation (fraction of day on 3/14).

- **`PiDigits.nb`** - Pi digits analysis.

- **`PiSeries.nb`** - Pi series calculations.

- **`primeProbabilities.nb`** - Probability analysis of permutations of consecutive numbers being prime. Analyzes numbers 1985-1995.

- **`Primes.nb`** - Prime number explorations.

## Q

- **`Qubits.nb`** - Fitting exponential model to historical qubit data (1998-2018). Shows growth of quantum computing capabilities.

## S

- **`SFactor.nb`** - Polynomial factoring package.

- **`Shooting.nb`** - Shooting method for solving boundary value problems.

- **`Shor_qubits.nb`** - Analysis of qubits needed for Shor's algorithm to factor numbers. Uses Reduce to find bounds on qubit requirements.

- **`Spirals.nb`** - Exploration of mathematical spirals (conical, helix, spherical, Seiffert) with 3D graphics. Large comprehensive notebook.

- **`SquareFolding.nb`** - Square folding visualization (includes animated GIF).

## T

- **`Thinking_depth.nb`** - "2/3 of the mean" game theory problem. Calculates optimal strategies for iterated reasoning. Shows convergence to Nash equilibrium.

- **`Totient.nb`** - Euler totient function analysis with recursive computation and visualization.
